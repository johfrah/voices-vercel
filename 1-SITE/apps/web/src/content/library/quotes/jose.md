José Santaella is a bilingual Voice Over Talent who has experience in acting on television.

He also worked on the radio and has been producing audio for commercial use for a few years now.

José is a native of Venezuela and speaks Spanish as his primary language.

He records from his home studio which had allowed him to lend his voice to wonderful brands, products, and clients from all over the world.
